The KRE is a

bold new idea

made

for

today’s

real

world.

The brilliant essence of Kin is the Kin Rewards Engine, an incentive system which pays developers a weekly reward based on a measure of the Kin user economy inside their mobile or web app.

Kin makes it easy for developers to stimulate users and create exciting experiences with cryptocurrency, all while generating new streams of revenue for themselves. The KRE drives the adoption of Kin as a currency through the creation of innovative use cases while also encouraging the exchange of value between users.

This exciting new alternative challenges the technology monopolies which have promoted the monetization of users by harvesting their data or injecting ads into the user experience – all with no benefit to the users themselves.

Kin is leading the Web3 evolution by empowering platforms and their users to forge mutually beneficial relationships. When it comes to creating an engaging user experience - Kin gives everyone skin in the game.
