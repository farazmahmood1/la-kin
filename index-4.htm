News & Events

We are thrilled to announce the launch of a new look, logo, messaging and website as part of the continuous development of the Kin brand. As the Kin Ecosystem moves forward in its mission to be a cryptocurrency used by millions of everyday people, our new brand identity better demonstrates the market-leading vision Kin brings to the cryptocurrency industry and the globe.

‍New Look

Kin has evolved since it launched some five years ago, and it was time for change.  The new Kin logo is a more modern look and captures our shared mission to deliver growth of the ecosystem and build the Kin future together.   The new icon represents many elements of Kin:  the icon has been curated to give the illusion of a cube, representing a building block.  It also can be seen to be an in-game gem and the gradient within the icon represents the letter K.  We have stayed true to Kin’s origins and kept purple as Kin’s main color.  However, typically you will see this color used with a darker palette.  

‍New Messaging

Our team worked with a leading brand strategy agency in New York to redevelop the Kin messages in order to sharpen them and make them appeal to a mainstream audience. Kin’s core messaging will focus on a voice that is bold, confident, street smart, and sociable.  

‍“Kin gives everyone skin in the game – making it easy for apps and brands to reward users and create exciting experiences with cryptocurrency, all while generating new streams of revenue for themselves. Tearing down barriers to crypto development, Kin is leading the Web3 revolution by empowering platforms and their users to forge mutually beneficial relationships. Win-win is a cliché – Kin is the reality.”‍

New Website

We are very excited to unveil the new Kin.org - your new destination for everything Kin.  Get inspired, explore Kin projects, discover Kin’s community and ecosystem, find events to connect with others and learn more about Kin. 

Some of the stand out features of this new web platform include:

  • A searchable directory of ecosystem projects and Kin NFTs.
  • A completely redesigned user interface and layout to enhance your online experience.
  • A case studies section that highlights Kin developer success stories.
  • More features will be continuously rolled out in the coming weeks, including new video content, a KRE earnings estimator and comprehensive ecosystem Dashboard to dive in further. 


This new brand identity has been designed to provide a better platform for all of our community members and developers to connect with others and advocate for Kin, while staying true to what the original Kin brand stands for.  

Of course, the task in the upcoming weeks will be to collaborate with ecosystem partners to update their Kin branding.  We realize unveiling a new brand can involve many steps and take time to finalize. 

If you use the Kin logo in any of your own projects,  please update it by downloading the Kin Branding Guidelines and ToolKit located here.  Note that we have allowed for creative ways to adapt the guidelines to fit with your own branding and Kin use-case.  Feel free to ask us if you’re not sure about something.

In the meantime,  we hope you love the new Kin branding as much as we do, and join us in celebrating the bright future of Kin ahead.
