Accelerator

Geared towards developers that have created high potential early-stage apps or services and have demonstrated the ability to execute.   The grant is unlocked in stages based on the achievement of measurable milestones where applicable.

  • 01
    Best for early stage apps.
  • 02
    Small upfront grant to get you started.
  • 03
    Larger upfront grants unlocked as your user numbers and Kin economy grows.
  • 04
    Available only to new apps and developers which apply before 31 December 2022.
  • 05
    Application subject to review and approval.

Content Creator

Grants available to community members to provide informative Kin related original content which is shared across social media channels.

  • 01
    Articles:  Write an article about Kin and post it to social media (500 words minimum including at least one graphic).
  • 02
    Videos:  Videos must be at least 90 seconds and posted to YouTube.
  • 03
    Content must be about the utility of Kin, Kin’s uses cases or apps, or the mission of Kin (i.e. content must not contain trading advice or secondary market information).  Content cannot be self-reported materials.
  • 04
    Apply for the grant after the content has been posted and provide the link.
  • 05
    Grants are up to a maximum of $200 in Kin. Award is based on approval and discretion.  The amount of the award depends on the amount of engagement to the article / video and quality of content. Limit of one grant per individual.

How to apply

for a grant

01

Application

Tell us a little about yourself, your experience and of course,  your pitch!   Be sure to include links to any MVPs or materials to support your application.  The stronger your application the better the chances of your success.

02

Application review

The Kin Foundation reviews grant applications on a rolling basis.  As your application is considered, you will receive updates via e-mail.

03

Feedback

We might ask to meet with you to discuss your application in more detail or ask you to provide further information.

04

Decision

Applicants will be notified via email whether their proposal has been accepted or not.
